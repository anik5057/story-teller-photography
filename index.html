About Us

Welcome to Story Teller Photography, where your moments turn into memories that last a lifetime.

We started this studio with one simple belief: the best photographs are the ones that feel real. Whether it's the quiet nervousness before a pre-wedding shoot, the joy of a wedding day, a child's birthday laughter, or the warmth of an anniversary — we are there to capture it exactly as it happens.

Our team blends candid storytelling with a creative eye, using the latest photo and video equipment to deliver a gallery that feels like your story, not a template. From Delhi NCR to destination shoots across India, we travel wherever your story takes us.
